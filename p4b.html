Problem Statements

Interactive Quizzes

It's tough to keep up with the latest cybersecurity threats. Users need engaging ways to learn about new attack vectors and how to defend against them.

Counter

We intend to implement AI to administer and evaluate quizzes, allowing users to quickly gain a vast amount of knowledge and improve their defenses in real time.

Malicious Email Detector

Attackers often spoof email addresses, making fake ones look real. Users lack tools to quickly verify whether an email sender is genuine. On top of that, thousands of phishing emails are sent daily, making it difficult to accurately identify real threats.

Counter

We intend to implement our custom AI embeded system to analyze temporary email accounts often used in phishing attacks. This way, users can easily identify phishing emails as well as gather detailed information about the suspicious address.

Malicious URL Detector

Fraudulent links mimic trusted websites. Users unknowingly click them, exposing credentials and personal data. With nearly 3.5 billion phishing URLs created every year, it’s impossible for users to manually verify each link.

Counter

We will introduce a browser privacy extension that performs thorough checks on the websites users visit. It will generate a real-time report before users enter any sensitive information, helping them avoid credential theft and malware traps.
