Abstract

This project introduces the Phishing Awareness Portal; an interactive educational toolkit designed to raise awareness and enhance understanding of phishing threats.

The portal offers three major features: a phishing quiz for training and evaluation, a suspicious email address analyzer, and a scam website checker.

All modules are browser-based and visually styled to attract user engagement while providing real-time feedback.

With phishing being one of the most exploited cyber threats globally, this toolkit aims to inform and protect users through hands-on simulation, interactive learning, and real-time detection mechanisms.
