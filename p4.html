Project Introduction

Phishing is one of the most widespread cyber threats where attackers deceive users into revealing sensitive information like passwords, bank details, or personal data.

These attacks often mimic legitimate sources, tricking users with emails or fake websites.

Despite being common, many people still fall victim due to a lack of awareness.

Our Phishing Awareness Portal is built to tackle this — by educating, testing, and helping users identify threats in real-time using interactive tools.

It's not just a project — it’s a mission to make the web safer, smarter, and more secure.
